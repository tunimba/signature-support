## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I create a signature?**
Open **Create**, enter the name you want to sign, pick a style, color, and optional mark, then tap *Generate*. You'll get several styles to choose from. Tap one to save it.

**How do I scan my own signature?**
Open **Scan**, take a photo of your signature on paper (or pick one from your library). Signafy removes the background on your device and gives you a clean, transparent version. Use the strength slider to fine-tune the cutout.

**How do I save or share a signature?**
From any signature, tap **Save as Photo**, **Save as File**, or **Share**. Signatures are saved as transparent PNGs you can use anywhere.

**Do I need an account?**
No. Signafy works without an account.

**How do I manage or cancel my subscription?**
Subscriptions are handled by Apple. Open the **Settings** app → tap your name → **Subscriptions** → select Signafy to manage or cancel. Cancel at least 24 hours before renewal to avoid being charged.

**How do I restore my purchase?**
Open the paywall in the app and tap **Restore**.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Saved signatures stay on your device, scans are processed on your device, and nothing is linked to your identity. See our [Privacy Policy](https://tunimba.github.io/signature-privacypolicy/).
